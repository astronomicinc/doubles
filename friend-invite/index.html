First time hearing about us? Here's the short version

A dating event that doesn't feel like one.

Doubles is a quarterly, hand-curated dinner for thirty single founders, investors, and operators in San Francisco. The format is what we call doubles — you came with one friend you'd trust to set you up. The night opens with a fireside chat plus a long Q&A (we call it the Volley), and rolls into an open-buffet dinner party that wraps by nine.

What it is.

  • Thirty curated singles in a private SF home
  • Catered dinner, hand-picked wine, one big shared night
  • A fireside chat & Q&A with a founder or investor
  • A chance to leave with a phone number, or at least a story

What it isn't.

  • A mixer or speed-dating round
  • Name tags or icebreakers or matchmaker clipboards
  • A pitch event — pitching is against the rules
  • An app or a swipe queue
