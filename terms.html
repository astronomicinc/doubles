1. What Doubles Is

Doubles offers exclusive, curated dating events for accomplished singles in San Francisco. Events are held at private venues and limited to 30 attendees. Participation requires approval through an application process.

2. Eligibility

To attend Doubles events, you must:

  • Be at least 25 years old
  • Be single and genuinely seeking connection or partnership
  • Meet at least one of these criteria: Post-exit founder, Active founder (Series B+), Executive ($200K+ annually), Angel investor or VC, or other (meet approval criteria)
  • Bring a single friend (of the opposite sex) who meets these criteria and whom you personally vouch for
  • Pass our vetting process

3. The Application Process

By submitting an application, you certify that all information is accurate and truthful. Misrepresentation may result in immediate rejection or ban from future events. We review applications within 48 hours and reserve the right to reject any application without explanation.

4. Your Responsibilities

By attending, you agree to:

  • Follow the Code of Conduct
  • Treat all attendees with respect and consent-first behavior
  • Not photograph or record anyone without permission
  • Not share attendee names or personal details outside the community
  • Represent yourself honestly
  • Arrive on time and prepared to engage

5. Pricing and Payment

Event tickets cost $110 per person. Payment is due upon ticket purchase. All sales are final and non-refundable, except as described below.

6. Cancellations and Refunds

  • Cancellation 14+ days before event: Full refund

  • Cancellation less than 7 days before: No refund
  • No-show: No refund, may impact future eligibility

7. Event Cancellation

If we must cancel an event, we will notify you and offer either a full refund or priority registration for the next event.
