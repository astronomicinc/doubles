The Volley

Where great conversations become great connections.

What is The Volley?

The Volley is a fireside chat series featuring world-class founders, investors, and operators. Every Doubles event opens with 30 minutes of real conversation—no keynote speeches, no corporate polish. Just a founder who sold a company for billions, or an investor who's backed unicorns, in conversation with our host, fielding questions from the room.

Think of it as the icebreaker you actually want to attend.

Why This Format Actually Works

External Focus

Most dating events put you on the spot immediately. In The Volley, everyone's attention is on the speaker. You get to listen, observe, and learn how other people think—without performing.

Shared Context

By the time dinner starts, you've all heard the same story. The same insights. Suddenly you have something real to talk about. Conversation flows naturally.

Character Signals

How someone asks a question reveals their values. Their curiosity. Their taste. You learn who matters before you ever have a one-on-one conversation.

Inspiration Over Awkwardness

You leave feeling inspired. Even if you don't exchange numbers, you've learned something and met ambitious people who challenge you. That's worth the evening.

Questions?

Do I have to ask a question?

Nope. The host will guide the conversation. If you have something to ask, raise your hand. If not, that's totally fine.

What if I'm not interested in the topic?

Come for the person, not the topic. Every speaker has a story worth hearing. Plus, you're there for the dinner and the people too.

Will there be a speaker at every event?

Yes. The Volley is core to the Doubles experience. Different speaker each month.

Can I request a speaker?

Absolutely. Email us with suggestions—especially if you know someone amazing.

Is there a cover charge for just attending The Volley?

No. The Volley is part of the Doubles event experience. $110/person includes the chat, Q&A, and dinner.
