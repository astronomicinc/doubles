THE DOUBLES MODEL

Why bringing a friend changes everything.

1.

YOU APPLY

Fill out a short form. Tell us about yourself. Why you want to meet amazing people. We review your application carefully—not algorithmically, just honestly.

2.

WE SAY YES

We approve your application. This isn't gatekeeping—it's quality control. We're building a community of serious, fun, accomplished people. You fit that. Now comes the fun part.

3.

BRING A FRIEND

Find one single friend of the opposite sex you'd genuinely want to hang with. It's that simple. They don't have to be a founder. They just have to be great.

4.

YOU BOTH COME

You and your friend show up. 15 other pairs do the same. Suddenly it's 30 people—all vouched for by someone in the room. No strangers. No algorithms. Just good people.

5.

WE MIX IT UP

Catered drinks, great music, and a relaxed structure. We kick things off with a few ice-breakers. Then you mingle. Meet people. Have real conversations.

6.

REAL CONNECTIONS

No swiping. No judgment. No pressure. Just people having a genuinely good time. Some will spark. Some will become friends. That's the whole point.

WHY THIS MODEL CRUSHES EVERYTHING ELSE

✓

Built-In Vetting

Your friend knows you personally. They're vouching for your character. That's infinitely better than an algorithm that reads a profile. If you're a dud, your friend's reputation is on the line. Suddenly, everyone takes it seriously.

✓

Gender Balance (Actually Works)

Dating apps skew heavily male. We guarantee gender balance because the bring-a-friend rule requires pairs. 15 men bring 15 women. 15 women bring 15 men. Math.

✓

Zero Flakes

When you bring a friend, you're both committed. You're not ghosting 30 minutes before. You're not "maybe" going. You're invested because your friend is counting on you.

✓

Lower Pressure Dating

You're not alone scrolling through profiles. You're with a friend you like. You're at a nice venue. If conversation stalls with one person, your friend is right there. It's social, not transactional.

✓

Actual Conversation

No opening with "How's your week?" after 100 identical Hinge messages. At Doubles, you're in a room with curated people having real conversations. Chemistry actually matters.

✓

Network Effects

You go once with a friend. You meet 14 other pairs. You both have new friends AND potential dates. You get invited to the next event. And the one after that. Community compounds.

Who Should You Bring?

Your bring-a-friend choice matters. Here's what we're looking for:

✓
Someone you'd actually spend time with.

This isn't a favor. You're both going to enjoy the evening. If your friend is there out of obligation, everyone feels it.

✓
Someone genuine.

We're not looking for models or Instagram influencers. We're looking for real, interesting, kind people. People you respect.

✓
Someone actually single.

This is obvious, but: actually single. Actually looking. Actually interested in meeting people. Not "exploring options while in a relationship."

✓
Someone fun.

They should enjoy good conversation, good wine, and good people. They don't need to be the life of the party. They just need to be someone who adds to the room.

✓
Someone you can vouch for.

You're putting your reputation on the line. If they're a jerk, flaky, or looking for the wrong thing, that reflects on you. So bring someone you genuinely trust.

Real talk: We get it. Finding that person can be the hard part. But think about it: if you can't name one amazing single friend you'd want to set up with cool people, maybe it's time to expand your circle. Your friend doesn't have to be famous. They just have to be real.
