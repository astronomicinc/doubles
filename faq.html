Questions?
We've got answers.

How do I apply?

Head to the apply page and fill out the short form. Tell us about yourself, why you want to attend, and who you'd bring. We review applications carefully and get back to you within 48 hours.

What if I get rejected?

We're selective because we want quality over volume. If we say no, it's just a fit thing—not personal. Reapply for a future event. We're always hosting more.

Who should I bring?

Someone single, genuine, and fun. Someone you'd actually want to spend time with. They should be serious about meeting people, not just there for the free drinks. Your reputation is on the line, so bring someone you genuinely vouch for.

What if I don't know anyone single to bring?

Reach out to us directly. In some cases, we can make an exception or suggest someone from the community who might be a good fit.

How much does it cost?

$110 per person. That covers the venue (usually a beautiful private home), catering, drinks, and the curation. You're paying for quality, not just drinks.

Can I pay per couple instead of per person?

Pricing is per person. $110/person × 2 = $220 per pair.

What's the vibe like?

High-energy but relaxed. You arrive with your friend around 7pm, have drinks and appetizers, do a few icebreakers, then mingle. By 9pm it's more casual—good conversations, good music, good people. By 11pm, whoever's still there is staying because they want to be.

Do I have to do the icebreakers?

Nope. They're optional. Some people love them, some skip them. Whatever works for you.

What if I don't meet anyone I'm interested in?

You'll at least make new friends. Worst case, you had a good night in a great space with quality people. But statistically, 40%+ of attendees come back, which says something about the experience.

Can I bring more than one person?

No. The rule is one person per attendee. This ensures gender balance and keeps the event intimate (30 people max).

Is this just for SF?

Right now, yes. We're SF-focused. We're exploring other cities for future events, but we're starting here because the community is incredible and the venues are world-class.

Can I cancel or get a refund?

Cancellations up to 7 days before the event get a full refund. Within 7 days, we keep the full amount (because we've already paid the caterer). No-shows don't get refunded.

Is this safe? What about vetting?

Yes. The whole point of the bring-a-friend model is human vetting. Your friend knows you. We also review all applications. It's not a random strangers situation.

Will my data be shared?

No. We don't sell data. We don't do matchmaking algorithms. You come, you meet people, you exchange info if you want to. That's it.

What if I want to host a private event?

We do those. Reach out and we can talk details.

Still have questions?

Get in touch and we'll help.
