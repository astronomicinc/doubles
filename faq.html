01

Applying to Doubles.

06 questions
Who is Doubles for?

Doubles is built for experienced founders, investors, and senior operators in SF who are tired of dating apps. The room is single, ambitious, and selective about how it spends its evenings.

We're not impressed by titles. We're impressed by people you'd actually want to sit next to at dinner.

How selective is it, really?

About one in four applicants are invited to a given volume. Most rejections aren't "no" forever — they're "not this room." We re-review applicants for every subsequent event and reach out when the mix is right.

How long does the application take?

Under three minutes if you've already lined up the friend you want to bring. Your friend gets a short version of the same questionnaire by email after you apply.

When will I hear back?

Within 48 hours. If we need a day longer, we'll email you. We don't ghost applicants.

Do I need a referral?

No, but they help — particularly for Vol. 01. If a current applicant or past attendee vouched for you, mention them in the application. We weight referrals heavily.

Can I apply solo and have you match me a partner?

Not for Vol. 01 — the doubles format is the whole point of the founding event. Starting with Vol. 02 we'll experiment with a small number of "singles seats" each volume, capped at four people.

02

The night itself.

05 questions
What actually happens at a Doubles event?

Doors at 6 with 30 minutes of welcome drinks & networking. The fireside (15 min) at 6:30, the Q&A (45 min) at 6:45, an open-buffet dinner party at 7:30, and we wind down by 9. Full timeline →

What's The Volley?

A thirty-minute fireside chat that opens every Doubles event. We hand-pick a founder, investor, or operator with a story worth sharing. No slides, no keynote — just real questions and audience Q&A. It's how we get the room loose without anyone having to perform.

What's the dress code?

Smart casual. Think: nice shirt, no sneakers, no full suits. The venues are beautiful — dress like you'd like to be photographed in the kitchen.

Are there name tags?

No. Name tags signal the wrong kind of event. You'll learn names the way you would at any good dinner party — by asking.

Are photos taken? Will I show up online?

We capture a few atmospheric shots — wide rooms, table settings, the speaker — but no candid guest photos without consent. Anything featuring you specifically gets your sign-off before publication.

03

Bringing your friend.

04 questions
Why do I have to bring someone?

The doubles format is what makes the night work. Everyone arrives with a wingperson they trust, which lowers the social temperature instantly. It's how we engineer "you walk in with an ally" instead of "you walk in alone."

Does my friend have to be the opposite sex?

For Vol. 01, yes — we're piloting the founding format heterosexual. Vol. 02 onwards we'll be running same-sex and queer-friendly volumes; you can opt into the volume that fits.

What if my friend doesn't get accepted?

Sometimes that happens — applications are reviewed independently. We'll let you both know, and you're welcome to apply again with a different partner. We do not penalize the original applicant.

Can two friends apply and bring each other?

Yes — that's the most common case. One of you applies first and lists the other; the second person gets the friend-side questionnaire and we close the loop.

04

Logistics & access.

04 questions
Where is it held?

In the homes of SF founders we trust — Twin Peaks, Pacific Heights, Russian Hill so far. The exact address is shared with confirmed guests two days before the event.

How do I get there?

Most guests rideshare. Street parking is available but limited; we don't have valet. If you'd like a ride home included, that's a perk for Founding Members starting Vol. 02.

Is the venue accessible?

We confirm accessibility on a per-venue basis. There's an accessibility field on your application — fill it in and we'll proactively confirm or reroute. We've reassigned a venue once already to accommodate a guest.

What about dietary needs?

Every menu has a vegetarian path by default. Allergies, kosher, halal, and other restrictions: tell us on the application and we'll confirm with the kitchen.

05

Money & cancellations.

03 questions
How much does a Doubles event cost?

Vol. 01 is $145 per person and covers catering, drinks, the speaker fee, and the venue. We are not for profit on the founding volume — we're building the room.

When am I charged?

Only after both you and your friend are confirmed. If either of you is declined, no card is charged.

What's the refund policy?

Full refund up to 7 days before the event. 50% inside 7 days. No refund inside 48 hours — but you can transfer your seat to another vetted single you'd vouch for, subject to our review.

Still wondering?

Ask us directly.

We answer every email from a real human within a day or two. If you'd rather just see the night for yourself, the application is the fastest path.
