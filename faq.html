Questions? We've got answers.

Format & Mechanics

What exactly is the bring-a-friend format? +
You apply individually, but when you're approved, you bring a single friend of the opposite sex. That friend also vouches for you (their reputation is on the line too). This creates a 2-for-1 dynamic: 15 pairs instead of 30 individuals, which completely changes how people interact. You arrive with your wingperson, not alone.
How many people attend each event? +
Exactly 30 people. That's 15 pairs. We cap it because it's small enough to remember faces and big enough to create chemistry. Bigger feels like a mixer. Smaller feels exclusive to the point of awkwardness.
What happens during the event? +
Check-in from 7:00–7:20 PM. Light mingling and introductions. Then we do some gentle mixing (sometimes structured, sometimes not). Catered bites and cocktails throughout. Curated music. The whole thing wraps by 11:00 PM. No name tags. No forced games. Just 30 interesting people in one beautiful room.

Approval Process

How do you decide who gets approved? +
We look for people who are accomplished (post-exit founders, executives, investors—people with taste), kind (no flakes or jerks), and genuinely interested in meeting someone. We also balance the room: gender balance, age range, professional backgrounds. It's not scientific. It's human judgment.
How long does the approval process take? +
We review applications and respond within 48 hours. If you're approved, you can register immediately and bring your friend. If we decline, we'll tell you why (usually just "not the right vibe this round") and you can apply again.
What if I get rejected? +
It happens. And it's not personal. Sometimes we're looking for someone who fits a specific gap in the room. Try again next month—we host regularly, and sometimes the vibe just lines up better at a different event.

Pricing & Logistics

How much does it cost? +
$110 per person. That covers catered food and drinks, venue, music, and all the logistics. If you're bringing a friend, it's $220 for both of you. Payment is due when you register.
What's included in the ticket? +
Everything: seasonal small plates from a local kitchen, a curated wine and cocktail selection, non-alcoholic beverages, music, and access to an intimate evening designed for connection. There's nothing you have to buy on top.
What's the dress code? +
Smart casual. Think: nice jeans and a blazer, or a dress, or tailored pants and a nice shirt. No sneakers. No t-shirts with logos. This is a nice house, and we're trying to be intentional about the vibe.
Can I bring someone I just met? +
The friend needs to be someone you'd actually vouch for. Someone you know, trust, and think is a good person. Someone whose judgment you respect. Not a stranger you just met. Your reputation is on the line.

Other Questions

Is there a waitlist? +
Yes. If an event fills up, you can join the waitlist. We run events regularly, and waitlist members often get first access to upcoming ones.
Can I go to multiple events? +
Absolutely. Some people love the experience and come back. We keep track of repeat attendees (we call them "doubles members"), and they often get early access to new events and venues.
Is this for single people only? +
Yes. This is for people actively looking to meet someone. If you're in a relationship, this isn't for you.
How do I ask more questions? +
Get in touch with us here. We read every message and try to respond within 24 hours.
