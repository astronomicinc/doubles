Why we exist.

Successful people deserve better options. No algorithms. No profiles. Just quality people, real conversations, actual connections.

THE PROBLEM

Dating Apps Don't Work

Algorithms are broken. Swiping is exhausting. Everyone's playing games. If you've built something, invested in something, or lead people, you know what quality looks like. Apps don't deliver it.

Singles Events Feel Desperate

Volume over quality. Strangers. Awkward icebreakers. Low-energy rooms. You didn't spend years building your life to spend your Friday night in a basement with randos.

Your Network Isn't Helping

Your friends want to set you up, but they don't know enough single people at your level. And asking feels... needy. So you default back to apps.

OUR VISION

The default way successful people in tech meet each other romantically — where the caliber of people, curation of experience, and intentionality of connection rivals that of venture capital, not dating apps.

1.

Curated, Not Algorithmic

Every attendee is vetted by someone in the room. Your friend knows you're serious. Their reputation is on the line. That's better than any algorithm.

2.

Premium Experiences

Hosted in the private homes of SF's most ambitious founders. Not restaurants, not venues, not generic spaces. Real places where real people live and build.

3.

Intentional Community

We're building a community of serious, accomplished people who want to meet each other. Not a transaction. A real network where friendships compound, and sometimes, so do matches.

OUR VALUES

Quality Over Volume

We cap every event at 30 people. Not because we can't scale. Because we won't sacrifice quality for it.

Intention Over Convenience

You have to apply. We have to say yes. You have to bring someone. This isn't easy. That's the point.

Real Connections Over Gamification

No swiping. No algorithms. No dopamine loops. Just people having real conversations and genuine connections.

Community Over Profit

We're building something that lasts. That means thinking about what's best for the community, not just the business.

THE STORY

Doubles started because I was tired. Tired of dating apps. Tired of the algorithm. Tired of swiping through hundreds of people to find one person worth talking to.

I'd sold a company a few years prior. I had friends who'd done the same—or were leading major teams, raising capital, building things. And we were all... stuck. Not lonely, exactly. But stuck in this weird gap: too successful for apps, too normal for matchmakers, too busy for organic networking.

One night, I brought a single friend to a casual dinner with some other founders. The person next to me? Same situation. Single, successful, frustrated with dating. We got to talking. Really talking. And I realized: this is what's missing. Real people, real conversations, in a real space. No algorithms. No profile gaming. Just chemistry.

That dinner sparked something. If I felt this way, how many other founders did too? What if we built something that solved this explicitly—not as a side hustle, but as a real service for people like us?

So I started Doubles. Thirty people. Curated. Intentional. Real. And it worked. People showed up. They connected. Some dated. Most made friends. All of them said the same thing: "Why doesn't this exist everywhere?"

We're building it.
