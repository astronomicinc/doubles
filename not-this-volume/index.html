Vol. 01 · A note from us

Not this room.
Not no forever.

Hi Jordan — we read your application, talked it through, and we're not inviting you and Alex to Vol. 01. We wanted to tell you directly, with a real explanation of what that means and what happens next, rather than a form letter.

Hold of $145 released today You're auto-reviewed for Vol. 02 No re-apply needed

Vol. 01 is thirty seats and the founding volume — the room is the rest of the year's compass. We curate it tighter than every volume that follows.

About one in four pairs were invited this round. The reasons we couldn't make a seat for you and Alex aren't reasons we can list line by line, because that's not how curation works. It usually comes down to balance — the mix of industries, the match of energies, the small-room math of thirty people who all have to want to talk to each other.

We want to be clear about one thing: this isn't a quality judgment. The applications we have to say no to in Vol. 01 are almost always ones we want to say yes to later.

What happens now.

Three concrete things.

  • The $145 hold drops off your card today. You may see it disappear from your statement within five business days, depending on your bank. Same for Alex.
  • You're already in the pool for Vol. 02. One application covers all future volumes. We re-review you automatically — no second form, no awkward follow-up email. Vol. 02 is in September; you'll hear either way by mid-August.
  • The Dispatch keeps you in the loop. Quarterly notes from the floor — what happened at the last volume, who's coming up, occasional love stories. We'll add the email on your application unless you'd rather we didn't.
Most rejections aren't no forever. They're not this room.

If you want to nudge us.

We don't take "but here's why you should reconsider" emails — not because we're precious about it, but because the curation logic is balance-of-the-room, not individual merit. There's no edit you can make to your application that would change Vol. 01.

What does help: between now and Vol. 02, if anything material changes — a new project, a different friend you'd bring, a move to SF if you're not here yet — reply to your confirmation email and we'll update your file. That actually moves the needle.

While you're here.
