Your picks are visible to no one — not the team, not the other person, not in any future audit. We compute mutual matches automatically, and only then introduce the two of you. If the feeling isn't shared, nothing happens. Nothing is shown. You can edit your picks until Wednesday.

01

Mark interest, two flavors.

Tap date on anyone you'd like to see again romantically. Tap connect on anyone you'd like an introduction to professionally. Both, neither, or one — your call per person.

02

We wait for mutuality.

Tuesday morning, we compute matches. Where you both said date, we tell you both. Where you both said connect, we make the email intro. One-way doesn't unlock anything.

03

You take it from there.
