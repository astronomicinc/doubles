Speak at Doubles

An hour. Thirty people you'd want to host.

If you've built something real — sold it, scaled it, walked away from it, learned hard things from it — and you'd like to spend an evening with thirty curious people who want to hear it, this page is for you.

The ask

One hour. Off the record. In a private home.

No slides. No livestream. No press in the room. Just thirty hand-curated founders, investors, and operators who want to ask you real questions.

6:30
15-minute fireside. One of our founders in conversation with you. Your story, your call on the angle.
6:45
45-minute Q&A. The room asks. You answer however candidly feels right. Chatham House — nothing leaves the room.
7:30
Stay for dinner. Optional. Most of our speakers stay. The room becomes yours to mingle in.
9:00
You're home by ten. Or you're at the after-conversation in the corner until 11. Either way, your time is yours.
