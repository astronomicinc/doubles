Short paragraph

Doubles is a curated, quarterly dating event for thirty single founders, investors, and operators in San Francisco. The format — what we call doubles — is that you apply with a friend you'd trust to set you up. The night opens with a fireside chat and Q&A featuring a world-class founder or investor, then rolls into an open-buffet dinner party. About one in four pairs are invited per volume.

Copy →
Long version

Doubles is the dating event we wanted to attend and couldn't find. We host thirty hand-curated singles — founders, investors, and operators in San Francisco — four times a year in private homes. You come with one friend who'd trust to set you up, and we review you as a pair. The night opens with a fireside chat and a long Q&A with a world-class speaker (a Volley), then rolls into an open-buffet dinner party that wraps by nine. Speaker is revealed at the door. Address is shared 48 hours before. No app, no swipe queue, no name tags. The room is the point.
