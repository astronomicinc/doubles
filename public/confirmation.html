How the night runs

Four moves, three hours.

6:00
Doors & mingling.

30 minutes. Upbeat jazz on the speakers, bay views in every direction, a drink in your hand inside ninety seconds. Find the familiar faces — or make new ones — before the room sits at 6:30.

6:30
The fireside.

15 minutes. Our speaker, in conversation with one of the founders. Story, lessons, candor.

6:45
The Q&A.

45 minutes for the room. Ask one good question — the next person at dinner will ask you about it.

7:30
The dinner party.

Open buffet, hand-picked wine, ninety minutes. The room moves — find the people you came for, leave the conversations that aren't working.

Show up

What to bring.

  • A real second question
  • A genuine answer to "what are you working on?"
  • Curiosity, not a pitch deck
  • Cash for the ride home (not strictly needed)

The kitchen handles the rest.

Dress

Smart casual.

  • No sneakers (smart loafers are fine)
  • Layers — the apartment is warm, the city isn't
  • No suits unless that's what you wear on a Saturday
  • Anything you'd wear to a friend's dinner
If something goes wrong

The founder hotline.

Running late, lost on the way, locked out at the door — text Di at (415) 555-2147. A founder answers within five minutes between 5 PM and midnight tonight.

If you need to bail entirely, also text — we'll handle Alex.

One last thing

Treat the room like a friend's dinner party. Be curious, be kind, take "no" gracefully, and tell us if something feels off — we'll handle it.
