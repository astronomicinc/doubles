The opening Volley

Maya Patel.

Co-founder of Kestrel · Now at Outline Ventures
She talked about
"Building a team you'd still want to work with after the acquihire."

Maya walked in five minutes before doors, gave Di a hug, and changed the energy of the room by herself. The fireside ran fifteen minutes; the Q&A pushed forty-eight. Three of the questions in that Q&A turned into dinner threads at every table.

If you've been to a panel where the audience disappears into their phones — this wasn't that. Maya called on people by name, asked follow-ups, and at one point sat down on the floor of the living room because someone in the back was struggling to be heard.

One story from the night

We've been dating six weeks. Neither of us was looking for it. We were both looking for the room.
