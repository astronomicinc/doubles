What happens next

Four moves. Two weeks, ideally less.

Every Doubles application is two-sided. Yours is in; Alex's is what we're waiting on. Here's the timing.

  • Right now Step 02

    Alex gets an invitation email.

    We sent it to alex@mendoza.studio in the last minute. It's short — your vouch, a quick "what is Doubles", and a five-question form. Their card gets held the same way yours did. If they need a nudge, send them this link: doubles.singles/i/8XK2Q

  • Within 7 days Soft deadline

    Alex submits their side.

    Most friends finish in five minutes. If it's been 48 hours, we'll send one gentle reminder. If they don't respond at all, your application stays in our pool — we'll just hold it for the next volume rather than this one.

  • 48 hours after Step 03

    We review.

    Two of the three founders look at both applications together. About one in four pairs come through — most rejections aren't no forever, they're not this room. You'll hear back either way, by email, within 48 hours of the second application landing.

  • If we say yes Step 04

    Your card is charged. You're in.

    We charge $145 each — yours and Alex's, separately — and send you a confirmation page with everything you need. We share the venue address 48 hours before the event. If we say no, the hold drops off your card within five business days.

We sent a copy of your application and this timeline to {{user_email}}. If you don't see it within ten minutes, check spam or email us.
