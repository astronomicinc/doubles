Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

What We Collect

When you apply for Doubles events, we collect:

  • Name, email, age, and location
  • Background information (founder status, income, company details)
  • Information about your plus-one friend
  • Newsletter signup information
  • Event attendance and feedback

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Process your application and registration
  • Send you event details, confirmations, and updates
  • Communicate about Doubles services and events
  • Improve our events and services
  • Comply with legal obligations

Email Communications

We use Mailchimp to manage our mailing list. By signing up, you consent to receive emails about upcoming events and community updates. You can unsubscribe at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link in our emails.

Third-Party Services

We use:

  • Mailchimp (email marketing)
  • Google Analytics (website analytics)
  • Vercel (hosting)
  • Luma (event ticketing)

These services have their own privacy policies. We are not responsible for their practices.

Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct or delete your information
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Request a copy of your data

To exercise these rights, email us.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy at any time. We will notify you of significant changes via email or website notice.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Email us.
