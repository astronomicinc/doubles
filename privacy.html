Privacy Policy for Doubles

Effective Date: June 1, 2026

Introduction

Doubles ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") operates the doubles.singles website and dating events. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our platform.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, including:

  • Name, email address, age, and location
  • Background information (founder/investor status, income level)

  • Event preferences and testimonials
  • Newsletter signup information

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Process your event application and registration
  • Send you event details, confirmations, and updates

Email Communications

We use Mailchimp to manage our mailing list and send newsletters. By signing up, you consent to receive emails from us about upcoming events and community updates. You can unsubscribe at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link in our emails.

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

We use third-party services including:

These services have their own privacy policies, and we are not responsible for their practices.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal information
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Request a copy of your data

To exercise these rights, contact us at hello@doubles.singles

Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for individuals under 18 years old. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting a notice on our website or sending you an email.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at hello@doubles.singles or visit our contact page.
