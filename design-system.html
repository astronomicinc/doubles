01

Color palette.

Restrained brand palette. Teal carries primary surfaces; coral is reserved for CTAs and accent words on light; ball-yellow is the tennis signal — accents and italic emphasis on dark only.

§ Tokens
--teal Teal #1B5A6B Primary brand. Nav, How-it-Works, headlines on light.
--navy Navy #1A3A42 Footer, editorial sections, page headers on Volley.
--cream Cream #F5F1E8 Page canvas. Always pair with --ink for text.
--cream-warm Cream warm #EFE9DA Secondary canvas — Volley spotlight, alt sections.
--coral Coral #FF6B6B Every CTA. Italic accent words on cream backgrounds.
--ball Ball yellow #D4F748 Tennis signal. Dots, italic accent words on dark. Never on cream text.
--ink Ink #14272D Primary text on cream. Almost-black with teal undertone.
--ink-soft Ink soft #3E5258 Body / secondary text on cream. Eyebrow labels.
✓

Do — italic accent words.

Use --coral for italic words inside a headline on cream. Use --ball inside a headline on dark. The system handles this automatically when the parent section is .section--cream / --teal / --navy.

×

Don't — ball on cream.

Ball-yellow text on cream fails contrast and reads as a highlighter mistake. If you need yellow on light, use it as a 7px dot, never as a typeface color.

06

Spacing & shell.

Three tokenized vertical rhythms. Container 1280px. Gutter scales with viewport. Sections always use the rhythm tokens — never hand-set padding.

§ Spacing
--container 1280px max Outer page width. Apply via .container.
--gutter clamp(20, 4vw, 56)px Side padding. Same on nav, footer, and every section.
--section-pad-sm clamp(80, 12vh, 140)px Standard sections on cream — Next Event, testimonials.
--section-pad-md clamp(90, 14vh, 160)px Dark sections — How It Works, Volley, page headers.
--section-pad-lg clamp(100, 16vh, 180)px Final CTA — only one per page, always lg.
section-head margin-bottom 64px Between section eyebrow/h2 and section body.

08

Rules of the court.

If a new page or component breaks any of these, fix the page — not the system.

§ Rules
✓

One nav. One footer.

Copy the markup verbatim from this page (or import a shared partial in your framework). If you find yourself adjusting padding "just for this page," stop.

×

No new font weights.

Use one of the six tokenized weights. Adding a 700-weight headline or a 300-weight body breaks rhythm between pages — that's the bug you're seeing live right now.

✓

Italic accents in headlines.

One italic phrase per headline maximum. Color comes from the surrounding section (coral on cream, ball on dark). Don't manually override.

×

No new CTA colors.

Coral, Ghost, Ink. Three variants. If the design needs a fourth button style, the layout problem is hierarchical, not chromatic.

✓

Ball dots on every eyebrow.

The 7px tennis ball dot is the brand signature. Every .eyebrow starts with one.

×

No emoji. No stock illustrations.

Inline SVG icons (1.5 stroke, 28×28) and real photography only. Cartoon tennis balls are not on brand.
